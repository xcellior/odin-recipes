Fruit Salad

Description

Ingredients

  • Oranges
  • Lemons
  • Brown sugar
  • Pineapples
  • Strawberries
  1. Make the sauce on the stove and let it cool
  2. Arrange the fruits in a container, then pour the sauce over them
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Cover and refrigerate to allow the flavors to meld
